# ⛰️ 不周山

> **⚠️ 风格统一：** Anime动漫风格 · 中国古典仙侠风 · 禁止混搭

## 场景概述

**位置：** 洪荒大地中央，天地之间
**来历：** 盘古大神脊梁所化，曾是撑天之柱
**现状：** 已倒塌，山体崩裂，煞气冲天
**氛围：** 苍凉、悲壮、毁灭与神秘并存

---

## 环境特征

### 地理
- **高度：** 连接天地的巨柱，山峰高耸入云
- **山体：** 黑色岩石，表面有裂纹如骨骼
- **煞气：** 血红阴雾从裂缝中升腾
- **残骸：** 山脚散布巨石，如肋骨如碎骨

### 天气
- **常阴：** 天空常被煞气遮蔽，晦暗不明
- **雷电：** 裂缝中时有雷电交错
- **寒风：** 刮骨寒风，带着混沌气息

### 声音
- **风声：** 呼啸的风声如泣声
- **回响：** 轻微的响动都会引发巨大回声
- **寂静：** 有时诡异般的死寂

## 三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Full Scene Front
> The broken pillar of heaven — a massive ancient mountain with a jagged broken peak like a fractured giant spine. Dark crimson ominous mist and energy rising from cracks. The stone surface shows fissure patterns resembling bone fractures. Scattered rubble at the base like broken ribs. A huge crack running vertically up the mountain as if split by a divine axe. The sky above is dark and turbulent. Epic scale, dramatic storm lighting, tragic and foreboding atmosphere. Anime style, cinematic composition.

### 侧视图 · Full Scene Side
> The sheer cliff face showing internal structure like giant spine bones. Ancient primitive runes carved into stone in places, barely visible. Blood-red mist flowing from deep within the cracks. The mountain's broken profile against the turbulent sky. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail.

### 后视图 · Full Scene Back
> The desolate backside with massive rubble scattered at base. Ominous deep crimson glow emanating from deep within the cracks, illuminating the surrounding mist. The rear view showing the full extent of the destruction. Anime style, dark red atmospheric lighting, horror atmosphere.

## 重要剧情点

1. **盘古之死**：不周山是盘古倒下后所化
2. **共工撞山**：水神共工怒触不周山，导致山折天塌
3. **云霄经历**：云霄曾在此处见证祖巫与妖族的早期冲突
4. **法宝遗迹**：山中有上古法宝残片
