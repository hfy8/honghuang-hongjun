## 场景概述

**位置：** 三十三天之上，天庭宫阙
**性质：** 妖族政治中心，统御万妖之地
**氛围：** 奢华、威严、霸气、金光璀璨

## 环境特征

### 建筑
- **宫阙：** 黄金白玉建成的巨大宫殿群
- **天门：** 金色巨门，高耸入天
- **大殿：** 帝俊朝会百妖的金殿
- **东皇宫：** 太一居住地

### 装饰
- **色调：** 金色为主，白玉点缀
- **图腾：** 金乌、凤凰等妖族神圣图案
- **灵气：** 帝俊太一太阳之气充盈
- **守卫：** 金甲神兵把守各处

## 三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Full Scene Front
> A magnificent celestial palace complex above the clouds. Massive golden gates with intricate bird and sun motifs. White jade towers with golden rooftops. Divine golden light radiating everywhere. Countless smaller palaces surrounding the main halls. Flying celestial beasts around the palace. Imperial grandeur at cosmic scale. Anime style, golden dramatic lighting, epic composition.

### 侧视图 · Full Scene Side
> Shows layers of palaces built on ascending terraces. Golden roof edges curving elegantly. Golden armored guards stationed at intervals. Flying jade pavilions connected by bridges. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail.

### 后视图 · Full Scene Back
> Looking out from the palace showing the vast cosmic view below. The imperial throne room behind with phoenix emblem. Golden armor and banners. Anime style, dramatic lighting, aerial perspective.

## 重要剧情点

1. **帝俊立天庭**：妖族正式建立天庭，自立为天帝
2. **金椅之争**：帝俊太一共同执掌，实为兄弟共治
3. **量劫伏笔**：天庭的建立拉开巫妖大战序幕
