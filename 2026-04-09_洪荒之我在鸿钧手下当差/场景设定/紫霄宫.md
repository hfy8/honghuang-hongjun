# 🏯 紫霄宫

## 场景描述

**位置：** 悬浮于三十三天外的宫殿
**特征：** 紫色云雾缭绕、宫殿通体由紫色玉石建成
**氛围：** 威严、神秘、超凡出世

## 三视图描述（3D Multi-angle）

### 正视图 Front View
> A grand palace complex floating above purple clouds in the cosmos, built entirely of purple jade, towering pagodas with golden rooftops, divine light rays shooting upward into the universe. Massive celestial scale.

### 侧视图 Side View
> Shows the palace layers stacked vertically, purple mist flowing around the foundations, the cosmic void below.

### 后视图 Back View
> The palace from behind showing the main hall's back structure, ornate Taoist symbols on the pillars, the view into deep space.
