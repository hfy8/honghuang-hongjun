# 🌌 混沌海

> **⚠️ 风格统一：** Anime动漫风格 · 中国古典仙侠风 · 禁止混搭


## 场景概述

**位置：** 天地未开之前的虚空
**性质：** 无序的混沌之地，先天元气的海洋
**氛围：** 压抑、窒息、未知、原始恐惧

---

## 环境特征

### 空间
- **无光：** 无日月星辰，伸手不见五指
- **无向：** 无上下左右之分，只有混沌
- **无氧：** 普通凡人在此无法呼吸

### 混沌之气
- **颜色：** 深紫色渐变黑色的旋涡
- **质感：** 如同行走在粘稠的浓雾中
- **危险：** 直接接触会消融肉身
- **声音：** 混沌中常有低沉的轰鸣

### 危险
- **混沌兽：** 游荡的混沌中所化异兽
- **空间裂缝：** 随时可能出现的时空裂隙
- **迷失：** 一旦迷路，永困其中

---

## 三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Full Scene Front
> A vast formless void with no ground, no sky. Swirling dark purple and black chaotic energy filling the entire frame. A single tiny human figure appears lost in the middle, barely visible through the swirling mist. No light source, yet the chaos itself seems alive and faintly luminous. Oppressive suffocating atmosphere. Epic wide shot showing scale disparity between mortal and cosmos. Anime style, dramatic lighting, horror undertones.

### 侧视图 · Full Scene Side
> Shows the dense swirling chaos energy like layers of thick fog. The human figure is barely distinguishable, just a small shape amid incomprehensible scale. The mist moves slowly like living entities. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail.

### 后视图 · Full Scene Back
> From behind the figure, showing only endless churning darkness in every direction. No landmarks, no reference points. A faint glow from ahead suggests a distant objective. Anime style, dark atmospheric lighting, isolation and despair mood.

## 剧情意义

1. **第1集**：云霄穿越醒来，第一眼看到的景象
2. **盘古开天**：混沌被斧光劈开，清浊分离
3. **修炼极限**：传说混沌海中藏有超越圣人的力量
