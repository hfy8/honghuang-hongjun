# 🌌 混沌海
> **⚠️ 风格统一：** Anime动漫风格 · 中国古典仙侠风 · 禁止混搭

---

## 场景概述

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **名称** | 混沌海 |
| **位置** | 天地未开之前的虚空，无上无下，无边无际 |
| **类型** | 先天元气海洋/天地未分之境/万物本源 |
| **核心视觉** | 无光之境，粘稠如浓墨的混沌气旋翻涌，渺小的人影在无垠黑暗中漂荡 |
| **色调** | 深紫/黑/暗紫为主，无任何光亮，混沌气自身泛微光如深海生物 |
| **氛围** | 压抑窒息、未知恐惧、原始孤独、深渊绝望、无尽虚无 |

---

## 空间布局

| 维度 | 描述 |
|------|------|
| **纵向** | 无上下左右之分，方向感完全丧失 |
| **横向** | 无边无际，无法用距离衡量 |
| **层次** | 混沌气密度不一，有的地方稀薄如雾，有的地方粘稠如固体 |
| **中心** | 传说混沌海中心有"混沌之眼"，但无人证实 |

## 建筑外观

### 混沌气旋

| 项目 | 描述 |
|------|------|
| **形态** | 巨大的气旋状混沌在翻涌，有大有小，大的直径可达万里 |
| **颜色** | 外层深紫色，向内渐变为漆黑，最深处有微光 |
| **质感** | 粘稠如浓稠的墨汁或液态，又似固体，穿行其中如涉水 |
| **运动** | 缓慢旋转，有时突然加速，产生毁灭性的剪切力 |

### 混沌碎片

| 项目 | 描述 |
|------|------|
| **形态** | 混沌海中偶尔有固体形态的"混沌碎片"，如漂浮的岛屿 |
| **大小** | 小的数丈，大的可达千里 |
| **颜色** | 颜色比周围略浅，呈暗紫色 |
| **危险** | 碎片边缘锋利无比，可切裂金仙之躯 |

## 重要地点详述

### 混沌之眼

- 传说在混沌海最深处
- 据说盘古开天前就在此处
- 有极微弱的光从中透出

### 盘古居所（传说）

- 传说盘古在混沌海中修炼，醒来后劈开混沌
- 云霄曾在混沌海深处感知到远古意志

## 三视图 Prompt（精细版）

### 正视图 Front View
> An infinite formless void with no ground, no sky, no horizon. Swirling dark purple and black chaotic energy in massive rotating spirals filling the entire frame. A single tiny human figure appears completely lost in the middle, floating amid the churning chaos, barely visible through the dense swirling mist. No light source anywhere — yet the chaos itself seems faintly alive and barely luminous, like a bioluminescent ocean in the depths of space. Oppressive suffocating atmosphere that makes the viewer themselves feel lost and claustrophobic. The scale disparity between the tiny human and the incomprehensible cosmos is terrifying. Anime style, dramatic lighting, horror undertones, hyperdetailed, cinematic composition emphasizing cosmic isolation.

### 侧视图 Side View
> Shows the dense swirling chaos energy like layers of thick fog pressing from all directions. The human figure is barely distinguishable — just a small shape amid incomprehensible scale. The mist moves slowly like living entities, occasionally forming brief vortexes. No reference points whatsoever — no up, no down, no direction. The edge of another massive spiral visible in the distance, both beautiful and terrifying. Anime style, clean lineart, hyperdetailed, dark atmospheric lighting, isolation and despair mood.

### 后视图 Back View
> From behind the figure, showing only endless churning darkness extending infinitely in every direction. No landmarks, no reference points, nothing but formless void. The figure's faint outline is the only thing breaking the monotony. Occasionally a brief flash of dark golden runes in the far distance, appearing and disappearing. A faint glow from ahead suggests a distant objective — but reaching it may take an eternity. Anime style, dark atmospheric lighting, extreme isolation mood, hyperdetailed, somber tone.

## 在剧情中的作用

- **第1集**：云霄穿越醒来，第一眼看到的景象就是在混沌海中
- **盘古开天**：混沌被斧光劈开，清浊分离，天地初开
- **云霄领悟**：在混沌海的经历让云霄领悟"顺成人逆成仙"的道理
- **修炼极限**：传说混沌海中藏有超越圣人的力量
