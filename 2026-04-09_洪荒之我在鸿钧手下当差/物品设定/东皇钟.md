## 外观描述 · 详述

### 🔯 整体形态

```
尺寸：巨如小山，约三人高，通体金黄
形制：钟形，穹顶如圆，口径可容纳百人
材质：混沌金精所铸，坚硬无比，触感如触碰太阳表面
色泽：通体金黄色，流转着熔岩般的太阳真火，热浪层层
威能：散发金红光芒，照耀三界，所到之处，一切崩解
铭文：钟身刻有上古天道符文，为盘古开天时伴生神器
```

### 🔥 太阳真火细节

```
火焰色泽：从钟体缝隙渗出，由内向外，由金黄到血红再到暗红
火焰形态：如熔金流浆，缓缓流淌，热浪扭曲了周围的空气
温度：钟体十丈之内，万法不存，金仙以下不可近前
威压：靠近即化为飞灰，稍稍靠近则唇干舌燥
```

## 物品三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Front View
> A massive golden bell artifact approximately 3 meters tall, hovering in mid-air wreathed in flames. Nine three-legged Golden Crows coiled around the bell crown at the top. Molten gold and crimson fire flowing through cracks in the bell surface, emanating intense heat and golden-red light that distorts the air around it. Ancient celestial runes carved around the bell rim, faintly glowing. The bell's substantial walls show intricate engravings of the primordial chaos being split, sun and moon forming. Anime style, dramatic golden-crimson fire lighting, epic scale, intense heat distortion effects, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 侧视图 · Side View
> Shows the bell's thick walls conveying substantial weight. Sun-fire flames licking upward around the base creating a platform of fire. Fine details of cosmological engravings visible on the bell's side. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail, side fire lighting, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 后视图 · Back View
> The back of the bell showing the central golden clapper bar suspended inside. Ornate celestial patterns and rune engravings on the back as well. Anime style, dramatic fire effects, epic scale, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.
