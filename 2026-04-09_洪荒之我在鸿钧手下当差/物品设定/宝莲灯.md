## 外观描述 · 详述

### 🪷 整体形态

```
组成：玉莲台为座，神灯居中
灯座：混沌青莲所化，玉质莲花台座，瓣瓣分明
灯体：透明晶玉所制，蕴含造化之光，内有造化神火
火焰：造化神火，柔和却有无上威力，不伤人而护人
尺寸：约一尺高，小巧精致
光彩：散发柔和的金绿色光芒，照亮万物
感觉：温暖、创造、治愈，如母亲的怀抱
```

### 🔥 造化神火详解

```
火焰色泽：金绿色，如春日阳光透过嫩叶
火焰形态：柔和摇曳，不似凡火烈烈，而如烛光温柔
火焰特性：燃烧时无声无息，却能创造万物，治愈一切
温度：温暖而不炙热，近身时如春风拂面
```

### 🪷 莲台详解

```
莲台色泽：青玉色，如青山远黛，含苞待放的莲花
莲台层次：九瓣莲花，每瓣各有造化符文
莲台底座：圆形须弥座，雕有山川草木万物之形
总高：连座带灯，约一尺二寸
```

## 物品三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Front View
> A delicate sacred artifact comprising a jade lotus pedestal with translucent crystal lamp hovering above. Soft golden-green divine light emanating from the lamp, resembling gentle flames that give warmth rather than heat. The lotus pedestal has nine petals, each carved with creation runes and auspicious motifs. Nine-petaled lotus form base with mountain and river engravings. The entire artifact radiates an aura of creation, nurturing, and primordial power. Approximately one foot tall. Anime style, warm golden-green glow lighting, ethereal and divine atmosphere, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 侧视图 · Side View
> Shows the layered nine-petal lotus pedestal base with mountain and river carvings. The crystal lamp's hollow interior containing spiritual flames within. Soft warm light diffusing outward. Anime style, high detail, warm side lighting, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 后视图 · Back View
> The back of the lotus pedestal showing intricate lotus root pattern engravings. The lamp's back detail with subtle flame glow. Anime style, warm back lighting effects, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.
