## 外观描述 · 详述

### 📜 整体形态

```
组成：一图一书，分合自如
河图：玄色玉板，刻有星辰罗列图案
洛书：白玉书页，载有天道推演之法
尺寸：约一尺见方
颜色：河图为玄黑，洛书为羊脂白
灵气：常有星辰光辉流转其上，如银河泻落
```

### 📊 河图详解

```
板面：玄色玉板，约一尺见方，厚约一寸
图案：板面刻有天上星辰位置，九宫格排列，如实地星象图
色泽：黑如墨玉，深处有星光点点，如夜空星辰
符文：星象之间有银线相连，那是天地经纬，气机流转
功能：可推算天机运转，推演过去未来
```

### 📖 洛书详解

```
书页：白玉书页，约一尺见方，数十页合一
文字：书页载有天道推演文字，字体非楷非篆，乃上古天文
色泽：白如羊脂，其上文字以银液书就，触之即隐
符文：书页边缘有八卦符文，旋转不息，蕴含天地至理
功能：可布天道大阵，辅助修炼，提升突破成功率
```

## 物品三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Front View
> Two ancient mystical artifacts floating parallel in mid-air. One obsidian black jade tablet approximately one foot square with an intricate star map pattern engraved on the surface — nine palace arrangement of stars connected by silver threads. Beside it a white jade booklet with pages showing ancient astronomical text in silver liquid script. Cosmic energy flowing between them — azure star light emanates from the black tablet, white divine glow from the white booklet. Stars and galaxies swirling around both artifacts. Ethereal starlight illuminating the surrounding space. Anime style, ethereal cosmic blue and white lighting, mystical atmosphere, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 侧视图 · Side View
> Shows the thickness of the jade tablets, the fine quality of the material. Cosmic energy flowing in visible patterns around the artifacts. The black tablet shows depth with stars embedded within layers of obsidian. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail, side cosmic lighting, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 后视图 · Back View
> The back of both tablets showing different pattern arrangements. The black tablet's back has a simplified eight trigrams pattern. The white booklet's back has jade-white pages with edge decorations. Anime style, cosmic glow effects, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.
