## 外观描述 · 详述

### 🌿 整体形态

```
树形：晶莹剔透的树枝，约一尺高，精致小巧
主干：通透的水晶主干，枝干分明
枝条：每枝各缀一宝，共有七枝，故名七宝妙树
宝石：赤珠、黄牙、青莲、紫玉、白毫、金莲、银瓶
色泽：七种颜色流转，霞光万道，宝气盈盈
光彩：绽放时宝光十丈，霞彩弥天，隐有天花乱坠之象
```

## 物品三视图 · 精细描述（Anime Style）

### 正视图 · Front View
> A exquisite miniature tree artifact approximately one foot tall, with a translucent crystal trunk dividing into multiple branches. Each branch tip adorned with a different colored gem: a red pearl, a yellow crystal, a green lotus flower, a purple jade piece, a white silvery filament, a golden lotus, and a silver vase. All gems radiating seven-colored divine light. The entire artifact emanates a soft prismatic glow, petals of light floating upward like a gentle rain. Ethereal and magnificent, like a sacred tree from paradise. Anime style, seven-colored prismatic lighting, mystical Buddhist atmosphere, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 侧视图 · Side View
> Shows the tree's delicate crystal trunk structure with seven branches at varying angles. The various gems at different angles catching and refracting light in different colors. Anime style, high detail, prismatic side lighting, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.

### 后视图 · Back View
> The back of the tree showing gems arranged on the reverse side as well, maintaining visual balance. Anime style, prismatic back lighting effects, Chinese xianxia aesthetic.
