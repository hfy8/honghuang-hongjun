## 人物设定

**核心性格：**
- **接引**：深沉内敛、忍辱负重、以大慈悲著称
- **准提**：灵活变通、不拘小节、以智谋著称

**背景：** 西方二位教主，紫霄宫听道时因门下弟子无蒲团可坐，准提用智谋为接引和自己各争得一个圣位，是书中的"灰色人物"。

**关键矛盾：**
- 处境最弱（西方贫瘠），却志向远大
- 常被其他大能轻视，但笑到最后

**关键台词（准提）：**
- "诸位道友，我西方亦需一席之地。"
- "小仗小，大仗大，此乃天道循环。"

#### 整体气质
灵活变通、看似温和实则精明、佛面含剑。整个人如同一只修炼千年的狐妖，外表温和无害，实则满腹算计，眼神中常有点点精光闪烁。

#### 容貌特征

| 部位 | 详细描述 |
|------|---------|
| **脸型** | 轮廓分明的俊俏男子脸，颔不宽但线条清晰，面如美玉，略带女相 |
| **眉** | 双眉如剑，漆黑短粗，眉尾上挑，眉峰尖锐，眉心偶有浅纹 |

## 三视图描述（3D Multi-angle）

### 接引正视图
> A thin elderly Buddhist monk with sallow complexion and deeply lined face like weathered wood, completely bald head, hollow dark brown eyes with profound wisdom. Wearing extremely simple and worn dark grey robes made of rough cloth, no embroidery or ornamentation whatsoever. Holding a string of wooden prayer beads in his right hand, the beads worn smooth from decades of use. His expression is serene and resigned, bearing the weight of suffering. A grey cloth sack slung across his shoulder. Standing with a slight stoop, radiating quiet dignity despite poverty. Dim but warm lighting from a single source. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail, realistic proportions, muted earth tones, Buddhist monk aesthetic, dramatic chiaroscuro.

### 准提正视图
> A handsome young monk in pristine white robes with a gentle calculated smile that hides sharp wit, fair complexion with refined features, short black hair in a simple topknot secured by a white jade pin. Clever twinkling eyes with hidden depths, often tilting his head slightly forward with eager calculated interest. Buddhist monk style with understated elegance. Holding a seven-colored treasure tree (Qi Bao Miao Shu) in his right hand, the tree ornamented with seven types of gems radiating soft Buddhist light. His robes are spotless white silk with subtle silver cloud trim at collar and cuffs. White cloth shoes with silver embroidery. Always appears slightly bowing or leaning in, an impression of humility that doesn't quite reach the eyes. Clean bright lighting with soft shadows. Anime style, clean lineart, high detail, bright color palette, cunning expression rendered subtly.
