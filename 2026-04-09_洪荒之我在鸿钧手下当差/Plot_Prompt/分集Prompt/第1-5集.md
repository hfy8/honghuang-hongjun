# 🎬 分集 Plot Prompt（第1-5集）

## 第1集：穿越洪荒

**Plot Summary：**
现代青年云霄一觉醒来，发现自己躺在混沌之中。四周是无尽的黑暗和狂暴的混沌之气，没有方向，没有时间。他不知道自己是谁，不知道身在何处，只有一个念头——活着。

**Plot Prompt：**
> A young Chinese man in modern clothes suddenly appears in the middle of formless chaos — swirling dark energy, no ground, no sky. He wears a torn modern hoodie and jeans. His expression: confused, terrified, then determined. The chaos around him gradually becomes visible as primordial mist. He starts walking upward through the chaos despite having nowhere to step. Epic wide shot, dramatic lighting, the chaos itself seems alive. Cinematic.

## 第2集：盘古开天

**Plot Summary：**
不知走了多久，云霄看到了一个顶天立地的巨人——盘古。他亲眼见证了开天辟地的全过程：巨人一斧劈开混沌，清气上升为天，浊气下沉为地。而他，被卡在了天地之间。

**Plot Prompt：**
> The massive figure of Pangu as a giant humanoid with long black hair wielding a divine axe, surrounded by explosive golden light as he splits the universe. In the foreground, a tiny human figure watches in awe from below. The cosmic explosion fills the frame — light, sound, primordial energy. The sky separates upward, the earth falls downward. Epic cinematic shot, golden lighting, scale contrast between divine and mortal.

---

## 第3集：紫霄宫

**Plot Summary：**
天地初开，万物诞生。云霄在洪荒大地上游荡，目睹了无数神奇异兽和先天神灵。他听闻三十三天外有道祖传道，便决定前往。一路上，他遇到了无数大能——帝俊、太一、十二祖巫、三清——他只是其中一个不起眼的凡人。

**Plot Prompt：**
> A vast cosmic palace floating above purple clouds beyond the mortal realm, golden divine light radiating from within. Countless figures in ancient robes ascending toward the palace on clouds and divine beasts. Among them, a single unremarkable figure in plain white robes walks calmly upward. The palace towers above all, mysterious and foreboding. Grand scale cinematic composition, purple and gold color palette.

---

## 第4集：三千大能

**Plot Summary：**
紫霄宫中，三千大能齐聚。所有人都在争夺圣位——只有圣位才能永恒。所有人的目光都盯着道祖身边的六个蒲团——那是天道圣位的象征。云霄知道，前世小说里，这六个蒲团引发了血雨腥风。

**Plot Prompt：**
> Interior of a grand hall in purple hues, over 3000 ancient figures seated on the floor, all eyes fixed on six golden cushions at the front. Tension fills the air — some figures glare at each other, others meditate calmly. In the middle of the crowd, the protagonist sits calmly observing everything. Wide cinematic shot, faces showing greed, fear, determination, divine light streaming from above.

---

## 第5集：道祖收徒（高潮）

**Plot Summary：**
就在众人争夺蒲团之际，鸿钧开口了："你，上前来。"所有人都震惊了——道祖竟然要收一个凡人为徒？主角在所有人的目光中走向前去，那一刻，他感觉到自己已经被卷入了一场无法退出的棋局。

**Plot Prompt：**
> An elderly majestic deity in purple-gold robes seated on a jade throne, surrounded by golden divine light and falling celestial flowers. He points one finger at a single ordinary-looking young man in white robes standing in the crowd. Every other figure in the hall turns to stare at this chosen one — shock, envy, rage on their faces. The young man himself has an unreadable expression. Cinematic close-up to wide shot transition, dramatic lighting, extreme tension.
